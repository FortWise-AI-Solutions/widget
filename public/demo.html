About This Demo

This page demonstrates the Alara Chat Widget in action. The widget provides a seamless way for customers to connect with your support team directly from your website.

The chat interface includes features like real-time messaging, typing indicators, and customizable styling to match your brand. It's designed to be lightweight and fast, ensuring it doesn't impact your website's performance.

To integrate this widget into your own website, simply add the script tag shown above and configure it according to your needs. The widget will automatically handle the rest.
